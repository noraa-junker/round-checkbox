In Switzerland where I come from it is very common to do voluntary work for the community in clubs and non-profit organizations. I personally have two very big commitments:

<h3>Jungwacht Blauring Schaffhausen</h3>

In the youth organization Jungwacht Blauring Schaffhausen, which I am part of since 2014, we offer weekly entertainment for kids aged 6-15. I myself was a group leader from 2020-2026. And also a department leader from 2022-2025.

In 2026 I started a new position in the canton board as the finance and technic chief.

More information: <a href="https://jubla.sh">jubla.sh</a>

<h3>Jugendparlament Schaffhausen (Youth parliament Schaffhausen)</h3>

In 2024 I was part of a group that wanted to reboot the youth parliament in the canton of Schaffhausen. I now sit in the board as tech chief.

This organization aims to make politics more young and interesting for the youth aswell as give them opportunities to express themselves politically.   

More information: <a href="https://jupa-sh.ch">jupa-sh.ch</a>
